- The Mission -

To provide the easiest way to navigate your desired destination in Malaysia.

About

On Easy Travel, I came to work every day because I wanted to develop a working website for my project. On this website you can easily find top destinations in Malaysia. You can browse the destinations from the homepage. However, if you want to view details of the destination you must login to the website. When you are logged in, you will be able to view details about the destinations. On the details page you will find the information about the city, where to visit, the map of the city and you can also find some hotels with price and a booking link. To login to this website, you must register. Loggedin users can change their username and password from their profile. If you forget your password you can ckick on Forget Password? link and type your email, the you will receive an email with instructions to change your password. You can also contact me through the website Contact page. If you have any question, fill up the form, click send and I will get an email.
